Make me laugh

Project Status: Incomplete

Software used: Unity

Language used: C#

about this project

Make me Laugh is a game where you play as a clown trying to get a job by making the interviewer laugh. Add different characters to create crazy interactions and see if your joke is funny enough to get you hired.

This game was developed in 48 hours for the Global Game Jam 2024, which had the theme "Make Me Laugh" It was created by a team consisting of myself, André Conde, Francisco Andrade, , João Macieira and Gil Monteiro.

Unfortunately, due to the numerous interactions between different characters, we were unable to complete all of them within the limited time we had. Additionally, the emergence of some issues led to the game being incomplete and having some bugs.
