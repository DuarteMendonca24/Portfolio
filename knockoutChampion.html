knockout Champion

Project Status: Completed

Software used: Unity

Language used: C#

about this project

Knockout Champion is a local multiplayer game where two players try to knock each other out of the map. Players must collect pickups that have different effects and try to avoid falling due to the game and map changes that will happen as the game progresses. The game has a duration of 5 minutes and in the end the player who knocks the other player more times is declared the winner

This game was developed during my time studying abroad at the Technological University of the Shannon in Ireland. It was created for the course "3D Game Engine Development" and the entire game was developed solely by me. The project follows the theme of the Games Fleadh 2024, "Start with nothing."

 pickups

The game has 3 unique types of pickups. A normal pickup (yellow) that the player can throw and if it collides with the other player, it will push him, a gravity pickup (red) that can be launched to the other player to make him loose gravity and stop moving and a shield pickup (blue) that gives the player the ability to push the other player further if they both collide with each other.

 map changes

The map also has its unique features, each 60 seconds 2 platforms will be removed from the map.

On top of that there are also 4 unique changes that will happen to the gameplay , the changes activated will be random and they are: no gravity platforms(if the player goes on top the platforms they will start going down) , inverted movement (the key to move right becomes the key to move left and vice-versa , also happens with moving forward and backwards) , increased jump and infinite ammo ( the players can throw infinite normal pickups ).

All this game changes will last for 15 seconds and will happen first at 30 seconds and then every 60 seconds.
